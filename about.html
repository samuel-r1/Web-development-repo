This is our story:

We are a small, family-owned restaurant based in Manhattan, New York, the capital of the world. We started with humble beginnnings, low funds, and a vision to feed the people of New York with delicious food and drinks. Our name, Breakfast Delight, stems from the feeling we wish to transmit to others when they eat our food, and for the most important meal of the day that determines how the rest of the day will go.

Our star dishes include The pancake platter, as well as The Egg and Cheese Omelette and The Breakfast burrito. These dishes carry out our homemade traditions with special, fresh ingredients. They provide a taste of the city with a taste of home, combined to a perfect mix of both worlds which merge into the city of Manhattan. We prioritize our guests wishes, including deals and plate of the day specials, so that all people who enter our restaurant have a dish to eat.

Our mission is our primary goal: to give delight and joy to every person that enters our restaurant, and to provide a warm and homey environment to all. In our 3 month history, we have worked towards that mission, and will continue to do so.
